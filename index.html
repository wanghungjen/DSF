Benchmarks

Accuracies per race, with overall Equalized Odds and Predicted Parity below.

Accuracy: For each race, how often the model (under the current toggle configuration) correctly predicts repayment success or failure on the test dataset.

Fraction Given Loan: For each race, the fraction of test-set individuals whom the model (under the current toggle configuration) predicts would be given a loan.

Fraction Given Loan (Original): For each race, the fraction of test-set individuals who actually received a loan in the real dataset.
